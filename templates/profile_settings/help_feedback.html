Help & Feedback

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I post anonymously?

When creating a post, the "Post anonymously" option is selected by default. Your identity will be hidden from other users, but you can still see your own posts in "My Posts".

Can I edit or delete my posts?

Currently, posts cannot be edited after submission. If you need to remove a post, please contact us through the feedback form below.

How do I report inappropriate content?

You can flag any post by clicking the "Flag" button. Our moderators will review flagged content and take appropriate action.

Who can use Tree Hole Yale?

Tree Hole Yale is exclusively for current Yale University students, faculty, and staff. You must use a @yale.edu email address to register.

How do I change my email preferences?

Go to Settings → Email Preferences to manage which notifications you receive.
